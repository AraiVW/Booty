    NCT

    We love music!

    NCT is a Korean Kpop band signed under SM Entertainment. NCT stands for Neo Culture Technology. The concept of their group is that there are unlimited number of members and sub-groups. Right now there are three active sub-groups.


    NCT 127


    Fixed group

    Made of 10 members (Mark, Teayong, Taeil, Johnny, WinWin, Jungwoo, Haechan, Doyoung, Yuta, Jaehyun)

    Debuted July 7, 2016

    NCT U


    Not a fixed group, members will join based on song concept

    Made of not fixed number of members (Taeil, Doyoung, Jaehyun, Taeyong, Ten, Mark, Winwin, Haechan, Jungwoo, Lucas)

    Debut day depends on member

    NCT Dream


    Nat a fixed group, Members join and leave based on age (only under 20)

    Active members are Haechan, Renjun, Jeno, Jaemin, Chenle, and Jisung

    Mark graduated december 31st 2018
